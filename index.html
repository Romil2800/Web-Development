Things to Do in Dartmouth

Shubie Park

Location : 54 Locks Rd

Activities : Biking, Walking and Kayaking

Summary

Shubie Park is a 16-hectare urban park in Dartmouth, Nova Scotia maintained by the Halifax Regional Municipality. Heavily forested, Shubie Park is roughly linear in shape as it is bounded on the north and west by Highway 118 expressway and on the south and east by Lake Micmac and the southern edge of Lake Charles
